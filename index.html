Features

Personalized Recommendations

Using data analytics and machine learning, the AI fridge provides personalized healthy recipe suggestions based on your dietary preferences and nutritional needs.

Efficiency Boost

With the AI fridge's ability to suggest recipes based on available ingredients and the integrated microwave tray's quick cooking capabilities, you can save time and energy in the kitchen.

Fruit & Veggie Washing Drawer

The AI fridge includes a dedicated drawer that automatically washes fruits and vegetables. This drawer is waterproof and utilizes a built-in filtration system to ensure clean water usage. The water supply is directly connected to your home's plumbing, and the drawer is designed with advanced waterproofing to prevent any leaks or damage to the fridge. Enjoy fresh and clean produce with just the press of a button.

Eco-Friendly

The FreezeWave AI Fridge utilizes Optane technology instead of HCFC, reducing environmental impact and promoting sustainability.
